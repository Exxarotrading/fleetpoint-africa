About Fleetpoint Africa

Practical fleet management for companies that need stronger control.

Fleetpoint Africa supports businesses that operate vehicles, trucks, trailers, plant, and equipment. We review your current fleet setup, identify operational weaknesses, and implement systems that improve cost control, compliance, driver accountability, and executive reporting.

We are built for companies that need experienced fleet oversight without immediately carrying the cost of a full senior fleet department.
